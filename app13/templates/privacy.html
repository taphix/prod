Privacy Policy

 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our company (hereinafter referred to as the "Company", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, processes and protects the personal data that we receive from our users (hereinafter referred to as "User", "you" or "your"). We attach great importance to the protection of your personal data and comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations.


 Data collection


We may collect and process the following types of personal data:


- Identification data: First name, last name, date of birth.

- Contact information: Email address, mailing address, phone number.

- Account Details: User name, password.

- Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, data on site usage sessions.

- Financial data: Payment card details and bank details (if necessary).


 Data usage


We use the collected data for the following purposes:


- Provision of services: Order processing, product delivery, fulfillment of contractual obligations.

- Improving the quality of service: Analysis and improvement of our products and services, provision of technical support.

- Marketing and Communications: Sending newsletters, notifications about new products and special offers (if the user agrees).

- Compliance with the law: Compliance with legal requirements, accounting and tax reporting.


 Transfer of data to third parties


We do not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:


- Partners and suppliers: Data transfer is necessary to fulfill our obligations to you (for example, product delivery).

- Legal obligations: We are required to disclose data in accordance with applicable laws and government requirements.

- Protection of our interests: To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Company, our users, or others.


 Data protection


We take all necessary technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction. This includes the use of modern encryption technologies, restricting access to data and regular training of our employees on data security issues.


 Data retention period


We store your personal data only for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, or in accordance with legal requirements. After that, the data will be safely deleted or anonymized.


 User rights


You have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability and objection to their processing. You can also withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.


 Changes to the Privacy Policy


We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. We will inform users about all changes by publishing a new version of the Privacy Policy on our website. We recommend that you regularly check the relevance of the Privacy Policy.


 Conclusion


We strive to ensure reliable protection of your personal data and their confidentiality. If you have any questions or concerns about the processing of your personal data, please contact us through the available communication channels.


This Privacy Policy comes into force from the moment it is published on our website.
