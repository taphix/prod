Cookie Policy for aviatorcase.cfd website

Effective date: 07/04/2024


On the aviatorcase.cfd website, we use cookies and similar technologies to improve the user experience, analyze traffic and personalize content. This policy explains what cookies are, what types of cookies we use and how you can manage their use.


 What are cookies?


Cookies are small text files that a website sends to your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. Cookies help the site remember information about your visit, which can facilitate subsequent visits and make the site more useful to you.


 The types of cookies we use


1. Required cookies: These cookies are necessary for the functioning of our website. They allow you to navigate the site and use its features, such as access to protected areas.


2. Analytical cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our site, such as which pages they visit most often and whether they receive error messages. This data is used to improve the operation of the site.


3. Functional Cookies: These cookies allow our site to remember your choices (such as your username, language, or region where you are located) and provide improved, more personalized features.


4. Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to display advertisements that are more relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of impressions of the same ad and to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.


 Cookie management


You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can configure your browser to block cookies or notify you when they are sent to your device. However, if you disable cookies, some functions of our site may not be available.


 Changes to the Cookie Policy


We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Policy on this page. We recommend that you review this page regularly to keep up to date with changes.
