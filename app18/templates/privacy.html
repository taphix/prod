Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Aviator company's website (hereinafter referred to as "we", "our" or "company"). Your privacy is of great importance to us, and we strive to protect your personal data. This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information. Please read this Policy to understand how we process your data and what measures we take to protect it.


1. Collection Of Personal Data


We collect personal data only with your consent and as part of the provision of our services. The personal data that we may collect includes:


- First and last name;

- Email address;

- Phone number;

- Postal address;

- Information about payments and orders;

- Information about the site visit, IP address, browser type and operating system.


We collect your data when you:


- Register on our website;

- Place an order or subscribe to our services;

- Contact us via the contact forms or by e-mail;

We use your personal data for the following purposes:


- Provision and improvement of our services;

- Contact you on issues related to our services and products;

- Analysis and improvement of our website and user experience;

- Fulfilling legal obligations and preventing fraud.


We undertake not to transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except in cases provided for by law.


3. Personal Data Protection


We take all necessary measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction. We use modern technologies and organizational measures to ensure data security, such as:


- Data encryption during transmission and storage;

- Restricting access to data only to authorized persons;

- Regular security checks and updates.


 4. Storage Of Personal Data


We store your personal data only for the necessary period of time to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, or in accordance with legal requirements. We strive to minimize the data retention period and ensure their relevance and accuracy.


5. Your Rights


You have the right to:

To exercise your rights, you can contact us through the contact details provided on our website.
