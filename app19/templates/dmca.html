 How to File a Copyright Infringement Notice (DMCA)


If you believe that your material has been used on our site in violation of your copyright, you can send us a notification containing the following information:


1. Signature: A physical or electronic signature of a person authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly violated.

2. Identification of the copyrighted work: Description of the copyrighted work that, in your opinion, has been violated.

3. Description of Infringing material: A description of the material that you claim infringes copyrights, and information sufficient for us to find this material on the site.

4. Contact information: Your name, address, phone number and email address.

5. Good Faith Statement: A statement that you believe in good faith that the use of the material in this manner is not authorized by the copyright owner, his agent or the law.

6. Statement of Accuracy: A statement that the information in the notification is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

 Counter-notification


If you believe that your material has been deleted or access to it has been mistakenly restricted, you can send us a counter-notification containing the following information:


1. Your physical or electronic signature.

2. Identification of the deleted material or the material to which access was restricted, and the location where the material appeared before its removal or restriction of access.

3. A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe in good faith that the material has been deleted or restricted due to an error or incorrect identification of the material.

4. Your name, address, phone number and a statement that you agree to the jurisdiction of the Federal Court at your address, or if your address is outside India, at the location of the company, and that you will accept a notice of legal proceedings from the person who provided the initial notice of violation.
