Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the website of the company engaged in the development and production of Aviator scooters. Your privacy and the security of your personal information are a priority for us. This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, protect it, and what rights you have with respect to your data.


2. Collecting information


We collect information when you visit our website, register for services, fill out forms, or otherwise interact with us. The information may include:


- Personal data: First name, last name, email address, phone number and other data provided by you voluntarily.

- Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, time of visit and the pages you viewed on our site.

- Cookies: We use cookies to improve the operation of our website, analyze traffic and personalize content.



3. Using Information


The information collected is used for the following purposes:


- Provision of services: Order processing, product delivery, technical support.

- Site improvement: Traffic analysis, user experience optimization, new features development.

- Marketing: Sending promotional materials, news and offers (only with your consent).

- Security: Protection against fraud, prevention of unauthorized access and other threats.


4. Data Protection


We take all necessary measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction. These measures include technical, administrative and physical security measures.


 5. Disclosure of information to third parties


We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except in cases where it is necessary for:


- Service Provision: Work with partners and suppliers to fulfill your orders.

- Legal obligations: Compliance with legal requirements, response to requests from government agencies.


 6. User rights


You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:


- Right of access: You can request a copy of your data that we store.

- The right to correction: You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

- The right to delete: You can request the deletion of your data under certain conditions.

- The right to restrict processing: You can request a restriction on the processing of your data in certain cases.

- The right to data portability: You can get your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

- Right to object: You may object to the processing of your data in certain cases.


 7. Changes to the Privacy Policy


We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. All changes will be posted on this page with the date of the last update. We recommend checking this page regularly to review the current version of the Policy.


8. Conclusion


We strive to ensure maximum protection of your personal data and transparency in their use. If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us through the communication channels indicated on the website.


Thank you for trusting Aviator Company.
