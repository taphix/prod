Privacy Policy

We respect your right to privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what measures we take to protect your information.

1. Collection and use of information

 1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when registering on our website, placing orders, subscribing to newsletters or contacting customer support. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and billing information.

 1.2 Non-personal Information

We may also collect non-personal information about you, such as data about your device, IP address, browser type, information about pages visited and activities on our site. This data is collected to improve the site's performance and analyze user activity.

 2. Use of information

 2.1 Order Processing

We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders, ensure product delivery and inform you about the status of the order.

 2.2 Customer Support

Your contact information can be used to provide customer support, respond to your requests, and solve problems that have arisen.

 2.3 Website Improvement

We analyze non-personal information to improve the performance of our site, optimize the user experience and develop new features.

 2.4 Marketing Newsletters

With your consent, we may use your contact information to send you marketing materials, including news about our products, special offers and events. You can opt out of receiving such newsletters at any time.

3. Transfer of information to third parties

 3.1 Service Providers

We may share your personal information with third parties who provide services to us, such as payment processing, order delivery, and marketing newsletters. These third parties are required to keep your information confidential and use it only to provide services on our behalf.

 3.2 Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legitimate requests from government agencies.

4. Information Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. These measures include the use of encryption, password protection and other technical and organizational means.

 5. Information Storage

We store your personal information only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Your rights

 6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to access your personal information and can request its correction, updating or deletion. To do this, contact us using the contact details listed below.

 6.2 Withdrawal of consent

You can revoke your consent to the processing of your personal information at any time. Please note that withdrawal of consent may limit your use of certain features of our site or our services.

 6.3 Complaints

If you believe that your privacy rights have been violated, you can file a complaint with the appropriate regulatory authority.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve its operation and user experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. You can configure your browser to block cookies or notify you about them, but in this case some functions of the site may not be available.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Policy on our website and indicating the date of its last update.

We strive to respond to all requests within a reasonable time and make every effort to resolve any privacy concerns.
