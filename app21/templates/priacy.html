Privacy Policy

 Introduction


This Privacy Policy defines how our company collects, uses, stores and protects the personal data of users who visit our website and use our services. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with applicable law.


 Collecting information


We may collect the following types of information:


1. Personal data: First name, last name, email address, phone number, delivery address and payment details that you provide when placing an order or registering on the site.

2. Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, data on site visits, including time and duration of sessions, pages visited on our site.

3. Purchase Information: Order history, preferences and product reviews.


 Use of information


The collected data is used for the following purposes:


1. Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and service.

2. Service Improvement: To improve the quality of service and user experience on our website.

3. Personalization: To provide personalized suggestions and recommendations based on your preferences and purchase history.

4. Communication: To send notifications about the status of orders, promotions, news and updates.


 Data protection


We take all necessary measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction. Including the use of modern encryption and information security technologies.


 Transfer of data to third parties


We do not sell, exchange or transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except in the following cases:


1. Partners and Suppliers: Who help us maintain the site, fulfill orders and improve our services, provided that they agree to keep your information confidential.

2. Law enforcement agencies: In cases provided for by law, when we are required to provide information at the request of government agencies.


 Cookies


We use cookies to improve the operation of our website. Cookies help us remember your preferences and improve your user experience. You can configure your browser settings to disable cookies, however, this may limit the functionality of our site.


 Links to third-party sites


Our site may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the content and privacy policies of these sites. We recommend that you read the privacy policy of each site that you visit.


 Changes to the Privacy Policy


We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. All changes will be posted on this page. We recommend checking this page regularly to get acquainted with the current version of the policy.


 User Consent


By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use our website.


 Your rights


In accordance with current legislation, you have the right to:


1. Request access to your personal data that we store.

2. Require correction of inaccurate data or their deletion.

3. Restrict or object to the processing of your data.

4. Transfer your data to another service provider.


If you have any questions or would like to exercise your rights, please contact us through the relevant sections of our website.


We strive to ensure maximum protection of your data and provide you with the best service. Thank you for trusting our company and using our services.
