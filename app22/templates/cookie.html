What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit websites. They help you remember your actions and preferences over time.

How do we use cookies?

We use cookies to:

  • Analysis of traffic and user behavior on the site;
  • Memorizing your preferences and settings;
  • Providing personalized content and advertising.

The types of cookies we use:

  • Session cookies: temporary files that are deleted after closing the browser.
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device until they are deleted or expire.
  • Third-party cookies: installed by third-party services that we use for analysis and advertising.

How do I manage cookies?

You can control and/or delete cookies at your discretion. For more information, visit aboutcookies.org . You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and configure most browsers to block their use.
