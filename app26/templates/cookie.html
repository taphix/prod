Cookie

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function more efficiently and provide information to site owners.

How do we use cookies? We use cookies for different purposes, including:

Essential cookies: Necessary for the proper functioning of the website, enabling basic operations like navigation and secure access. The site cannot function correctly without these cookies.

Analytical cookies: Collect anonymous data to help us understand how visitors interact with our site, allowing us to make improvements.

Marketing cookies: Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant ads.

Your choices regarding cookies You can accept or decline cookies. Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can change your settings to decline them. This may affect your ability to use some features of the site.

Third-party cookies We may use third-party services, such as Google Analytics, that use cookies to help us analyze site usage and enhance the user experience.

Updates to our Cookie Policy Our Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.
