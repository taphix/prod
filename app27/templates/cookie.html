Cookie

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites operate more effectively and provide information to the site owners.

How do we use cookies? Our use of cookies includes:

Essential cookies: Necessary for the proper functioning of the website, enabling basic functions like navigation and secure area access. The site cannot function properly without these cookies.

Analytical cookies: Collect anonymous information to help us understand visitor interactions and improve the website.

Marketing cookies: Track visitors across websites to display relevant ads.

Your choices regarding cookies You can accept or decline cookies. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can change your settings to decline them. This may restrict some website functionality.

Third-party cookies We may use third-party services, such as Google Analytics, that use cookies to help us analyze site usage and enhance the user experience.

Updates to our Cookie Policy Our Cookie Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated "last updated" date.
