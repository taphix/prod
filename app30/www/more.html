 Launch of a new line of mini-consoles “Aviator Ultra”

 

We are proud to announce the launch of a new line of Aviator Ultra mini consoles. These devices have even more powerful features and improved design. They are designed for true connoisseurs of high-quality games and provide a unique gaming experience. The new line uses the latest processors and graphics cards, which allows you to enjoy games in the highest resolution and without delay.

 

Partnership with leading game developers

 

Our company has signed a partnership agreement with several leading game developers. This will allow us to offer exclusive games for our Aviator mini-consoles. We are sure that the new games will delight our users and give them many hours of exciting gameplay.

Participation in the International Electronics Exhibition

We are pleased to announce that our company will participate in the international electronics exhibition, which will be held next month. We will present our new products and share our plans for the future. We invite everyone to visit our stand and learn more about our products and services.

New items


"Aviator Ultra"

As already mentioned, the new Aviator Ultra mini console line is our flagship product. These devices have advanced features and offer unsurpassed gaming quality. We have added support for the latest technologies such as ray tracing and VR, which allows you to plunge into the world of the game with your head.


"Aviator Lite"

For those who are looking for more affordable solutions, we have developed the Aviator Lite line. These mini consoles have basic functions and are suitable for casual players. They are compact, lightweight and easy to use, while offering excellent game quality.


"Aviator Retro"

Especially for fans of retro games, we have created a mini-console "Aviator Retro". It supports classic games of the 80s and 90s, allowing you to plunge into the atmosphere of the past. We have paid special attention to the design to create an authentic look reminiscent of the original consoles.
