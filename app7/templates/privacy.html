Aviator's Privacy Policy

 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes the procedure for collecting, using, storing and protecting personal data of users of the Aviator website. We understand the importance of confidentiality and security of our customers' personal information and are committed to protecting your data in accordance with applicable law.

 Collection Of Personal Data

We may collect and process the following personal data:

1. Contact Information

- Name

- Email Address

   - Phone number

2. Company Information

- Company Name

   - Legal address

   - INN and other details

3. Technical information

- IP address

   - Browser type and version

   - Time and date of the site visit

4. Cookies

   - We use cookies to improve the functionality of the site and provide personalized content.

 Use Of Personal Data

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

1. Provision of services

   - Processing customer requests and orders

- Providing information about our services and products

2. Improving the site

- Analyzing user behavior to improve content and navigation

3. Marketing

- Sending advertising and information materials (with the user's consent)

4. Legal objectives

- Compliance with legal requirements and protection of our rights and interests

 Transfer Of Personal Data To Third Parties

We do not share users' personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

1. With your consent

- If you have your explicit consent to the transfer of data.

2. Service Providers

   - Companies that provide us with the services necessary for the operation of the site and the fulfillment of orders (for example, hosting providers).

3. Legal requirements

- If it is necessary to comply with the requirements of legislation or court decisions.

 Protection Of Personal Data

We take all necessary measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction. Such measures include:

1. Technical measures

- Use of data encryption

   - Software update and support

2. Organizational measures

   - Restriction of access to personal data of employees

- Training of employees in data protection methods

 Storage Of Personal Data

We store personal data only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes of their processing, or for a period established by law. After that, the data will be deleted or depersonalized.

 User Rights

You have the right to:

1. Access to your data

   - Getting information about what data is stored about you and how it is processed.

2. Correction of data

   - Request to change or update your personal data.

3. Data Deletion

- A request to delete your personal data if it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected.

4. Limitation of processing

   - A request to restrict the processing of your personal data in certain cases.

5. Withdrawal of consent

- Withdrawal of your consent to data processing at any time.
